Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

What We Collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect your name and phone number. That's it. No email address, no payment info, nothing else.

How It's Used

Your name and phone number are used for one purpose only: to send you an SMS notification when a booking slot opens up. We use Twilio to send these messages.

We will never contact you for anything unrelated to appointment availability.

Data Sharing

Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with any third party for marketing or any other purpose. The only external service that touches your phone number is Twilio, used solely to deliver SMS messages.

How to Opt Out

You can opt out at any time in two ways:

Reply STOP to any SMS you receive — you'll be removed from the list immediately.

Contact us directly at parilthebarber@gmail.com and we'll remove your information manually.

Data Retention

Your information stays on the waitlist until you opt out or are removed. We don't retain your data after removal.

Questions

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, reach out at parilthebarber@gmail.com.
